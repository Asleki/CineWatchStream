Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions.

How can I find movies playing in a specific cinema?

You can use our Cinema Guide page to find movies in a specific location. Simply select your country and city from the dropdown menus to see a list of all movies showing in nearby cinema halls.

Is it possible to book tickets directly on your website?

Yes, you can! Our website provides links to our ticketing partners. Once you find a movie and a showtime you like, you'll be directed to a secure page to complete your booking. We are committed to a seamless process for you.

How do you get movie and show information?

We source our movie and TV show data from a variety of reliable APIs, including The Movie Database (TMDb). This ensures our listings, ratings, and details are as accurate and up-to-date as possible.

What should I do if I find an error on the site?

We appreciate your help in keeping our site accurate. Please visit our Contact Us page and send us a message with the details of the error. We will investigate and correct it as soon as possible.

Can I report a copyright or trademark infringement?

Yes, we take intellectual property rights seriously. Our official policy on reporting infringements is detailed on our Infringement Policy page. Please follow the instructions there to submit a claim.
