The Genesis: A Spark in Machakos

It was a sweltering afternoon in Mlolongo, Machakos County, back in 2021. The internet was buzzing, but quality local content often remained siloed, inaccessible, or overshadowed. Our founder, Alex Malunda, a self-proclaimed film buff and a budding developer, found himself pondering a simple question:

"Why can't we have a seamless platform that celebrates both global blockbusters and the rich tapestry of African storytelling, accessible to everyone, everywhere?"

From this question, CineWatch was conceived. Not in a Silicon Valley garage, but amidst the vibrant energy of a Kenyan town, fueled by chai, late-night coding sessions, and an unshakeable belief in the power of stories.

The name "CineWatch" itself is a blend of "Cinema" – the art form we adore – and "Watch," signifying not just viewing, but also guardianship over quality content. It’s a promise to both entertain and champion the creators.

Initially, it was just Alex, a laptop, and a dream. The first server was a repurposed old PC, humming tirelessly in a corner. The early days were filled with hilarious debugging sessions, accidental data wipes, and the occasional power outage that threatened to derail everything. Yet, each challenge only strengthened the resolve.

Why CineWatch? Beyond the Screen

Community First

We believe in the power of shared experiences. CineWatch fosters a community where film lovers connect, discuss, and discover.

Empowering Creators

We provide a platform for emerging African filmmakers, ensuring fair compensation and global visibility for their invaluable work.

Innovation & Accessibility

Constantly evolving our technology to ensure seamless streaming across devices, even with varying internet speeds in different regions.

Funny Fact: The Popcorn Debacle!

During a crucial early server upgrade, our entire office's internet went down. Turns out, someone had plugged a high-wattage popcorn maker into the same circuit. We now have a dedicated "popcorn circuit" and laugh about it over every movie night!
